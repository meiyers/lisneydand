Mei Lin Meyers is an artist. They have watched Mamma Mia! (2008) an approximated 50 times.
Mei Lin Meyers was born in 2002, into a family. At an age before she can remember Meyers was given a yellow book decorated with brown and purple spots. It was curious.
Here Mei Lin was introduced to the great Renaissance man, Folin Cirth. Folin Cirth was and still is shrounded in mystery!
The man, her biological father is an artist. Cirth is a painter of theory, the writer of on the nose facebook posts
an alum to every university and product of every institution!
Mei Lin was born of mystery! Conceived in Europe by Meryl Larx and Folin Cirth(?). Daughter to mother Meryl and dad Steve
Raised in a white household Mei Lin thought she was adopted. Folin Cirth was off reinventing the wheel. Meryl taught children
whereas Steve sold plates.
They grew up in the elusive Hills. Born of mystery and Westmead Children's Hospital!!
Mei Lin is currently a Fine Arts student at UNSW Art & Design (COFA - Corruption of Fine Artists) and is titilated by soft sculpture.
According to the internet Mei Lin means jade tree, plum tree and according to Meryl 'pretty'. The institutions (THE INSTITUTIONS!)
are unaccustomed to 'Mei Lin'. Centerlink (wrong - 'Mei-Lin') Medicare (wrong - 'Mei-Lin') Service NSW (wrong 'Mei' (middle name Lin))
HCF (wrong - 'Mei-Lin') USI (wrong 'Mei-Lin')
Until 2020 Mei Lin (in all these forms), did not exist; excluded from the system (THE INSTITUTIONS!!) by spelling mistake.
Until 2020 Mei Lin's health care records have been wrong.
But never fear; Mei Lin is related to the Rennaisance man. Nature and a need to explore the self (in a quite frankly obsessive manner)
has made them an artist.
A sick artist. A maker of plates, playlists and tentacles. Her great grandfather was supposedly a brilliant writer based in Hangzhou.
