Here's Back to the start

This fixation reveals itself at 1:26am.

A yellow book with spots, brown and purple. Very 2000s reject shop. The curdling of the spine, the adhesive now rendered useless.
I wish my grandpa was here to fix it. Stick it back together. I am attracted to the unknown, even as I know that the image I create,
the fiction will be more resolved than the reality. It is never the right time. Instead, I am caught up with fables, dancing with Xi Hu.
A fraud in a raincoat. Telling too much, never too little. I feel the most when I am stressed. I am faster, warm, nauseous.
Are these the butterflies in my stomach, Liang Shangbo and Zhu Yingtai? The state of overwhelm.
Shame as I search.
